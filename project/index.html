LET'S BUILD A NATION TO SAVE LIVES

Established in 2000.It aims to help poor children and provide them with best education so that they can grow and make their future bright. We closely work with government to identify academically weaker students and help them to ehance their skills.

About Us

It was established in 2000 when a group of friends came together with the intention to help poor children and giving back to the society. They were inspired by the people of other NGOs. Seeing millions of students havin no access to school encouraged the young minds to come forward and help them with the best they can.
